**Description:**
Blockers refer to obstacles or challenges that hinder the progress of a project. They can arise from various sources, including technical issues, communication breakdowns, and resource constraints. Addressing blockers is essential for project success, as unresolved blockers can lead to significant delays and cost overruns.
