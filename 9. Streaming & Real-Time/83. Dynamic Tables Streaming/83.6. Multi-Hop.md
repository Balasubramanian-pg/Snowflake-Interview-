# Multi-Hop Networking in Snowflake

**Description:**
Multi-hop networking is a technique used in network architecture to transmit data through multiple intermediate nodes to reach its final destination. This approach enhances network reliability and coverage, especially in scenarios where direct communication between nodes is not feasible. It is commonly used in wireless sensor networks, ad hoc networks, or any communication system that requires data to be routed through multiple hops.
