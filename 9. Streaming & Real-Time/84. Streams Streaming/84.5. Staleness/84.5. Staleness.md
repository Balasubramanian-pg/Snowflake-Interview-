# Staleness in Data Management

**Description:**
Staleness occurs when data becomes outdated due to infrequent updates, lack of synchronization, or failure to account for changing conditions. This can lead to financial losses, compromised decision-making, and decreased system efficiency. Understanding staleness is essential for data-driven systems, caching mechanisms, and applications where data freshness is critical.
