# Fan Out in Distributed Systems and Data Processing

**Description:**
Fan out is a technique used in distributed systems and data processing to improve scalability and performance by distributing workload across multiple components. It helps in achieving linear scalability but requires careful planning to manage complexity and resource utilization. Effective fan out management balances distribution to minimize bottlenecks and maximize throughput.
