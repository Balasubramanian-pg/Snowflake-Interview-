# Understanding Overhead in Business Operations

**Description:**
Overhead includes costs like rent, utilities, and administrative salaries that are not directly related to the production of a specific product or service. Understanding and managing overhead is crucial for maintaining profitability and efficiency in any project or operational setup. This concept helps in accurate cost allocation and pricing strategies.
