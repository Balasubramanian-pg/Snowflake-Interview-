# Understanding and Managing Contention in Hybrid Systems

**Description:**
Contention arises in systems where multiple components or users vie for shared resources such as databases, networks, or memory. Mismanaging contention can lead to significant performance drops, increased latency, and even system crashes. Understanding contention is vital for system administrators, developers, and engineers to troubleshoot performance issues and optimize system scalability and reliability.
