# Fail Safe in System Design

**Description:**
A fail safe is a critical component in system design that prevents or mitigates failures. It is designed to automatically activate in case of a failure, ensuring the system remains safe and operational. Fail safes can be active or passive and are crucial in high-stakes applications.
